What is NLP?

Neuro Linguistic Programming was originally developed by Richard Bandler & John Grinder who wanted to identify patterns of thoughts & behaviours of successful individuals & teach them to others.

NLP works using active imagination, where the client has to follow instructions and actively imagine what is being suggested
